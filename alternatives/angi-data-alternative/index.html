Angi/HomeAdvisor Alternative for Contractor Intelligence

Alternative to Angi and HomeAdvisor for contractor data. TradeBridge provides outreach-ready contacts, not a marketplace.

Why Angi's contractor data isn't available for outreach

Angi (formerly HomeAdvisor) has one of the largest contractor databases in America. Over 3 million contractors across every trade and geography. The problem: Angi is a marketplace, not a data provider. They connect homeowners with contractors. They don't sell the underlying contact data.

You can see contractor profiles on Angi's website. You can't export them, filter them by license status, or get verified email addresses and direct phone numbers for outreach.

What TradeBridge provides instead

TradeBridge builds contractor contact lists from state licensing boards and delivers them as clean CSV files you can use in any CRM or outreach tool. Every record includes the owner's name, verified email, direct phone, company address, license number, and trade classification.

Where Angi gates their data behind a marketplace, TradeBridge gives you the data directly. No marketplace intermediary. No lead fees per connection. Your data, delivered to you, for your team to use however they need.

How the data compares

Angi's data strengths include customer reviews, project photos, and homeowner engagement metrics. These are valuable for consumer-facing applications but irrelevant for B2B outreach.

TradeBridge's strengths are what B2B sales teams need: verified email addresses with 98% deliverability, direct phone numbers for business owners (not dispatch), active license status from state boards, and trade-specific segmentation. This is outreach-ready data, not marketplace data.

Per-project pricing vs marketplace fees

Angi's business model charges contractors per lead. Homeowners search for contractors; Angi charges the contractor for each connection. This creates an incentive structure that doesn't align with B2B data needs.

TradeBridge charges per verified contact on a project basis. Buy a list, use it for outreach. No recurring fees, no per-lead charges, no marketplace in the middle. Your data, your outreach, your results.
