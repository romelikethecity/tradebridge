ZoomInfo Alternative for Contractor Data

Looking for a ZoomInfo alternative for contractor data? TradeBridge delivers license-verified contacts without enterprise pricing.

Why teams look for ZoomInfo alternatives for contractor data

ZoomInfo is the market leader in B2B contact data. For selling SaaS to tech companies, it works. For reaching licensed home services contractors, it doesn't.

The core problem: ZoomInfo categorizes businesses using SIC and NAICS codes. Contractors get lumped under "Construction," "Home Improvement," or "Specialty Trade." These categories include lumber yards, hardware stores, interior designers, and building material suppliers. There's no way to filter for "licensed residential HVAC contractors in Texas."

The secondary problem: ZoomInfo costs $15K-$30K per year with an annual contract. That's enterprise pricing for a tool that can't do the basic filtering your contractor outreach needs.

What TradeBridge does differently

TradeBridge sources contractor data from state licensing boards. This is the authoritative source for who holds an active contractor license. We don't scrape business directories or guess at industry codes.

We segment by 12 trade categories and 26+ subtypes. HVAC alone breaks into residential, commercial, installation, maintenance, ductwork, refrigeration, and heat pump. You target the exact contractors your product serves.

Pricing is per project. A list of 3,000 verified HVAC contractors in the Southeast costs $900-$2,250. No annual contract. No platform. CSV to your inbox in 3-5 business days.

When ZoomInfo still makes sense

If your team sells to multiple verticals (not just contractors), ZoomInfo's breadth across industries has value. If you need intent data, website visitor identification, or built-in sales engagement tools, ZoomInfo offers capabilities TradeBridge doesn't.

But if your primary need is reaching licensed contractors with accurate, trade-segmented contact data, TradeBridge is purpose-built for exactly that.
