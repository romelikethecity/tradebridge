Contractor Data for Franchise Networks

Recruit independent contractors into your network.

The problem

You're recruiting independent contractors to join your franchise brand. But your development team is working from purchased lists that include chain locations, inactive businesses, and unlicensed operators. Half their calls are dead ends.

How TradeBridge solves this

TradeBridge gives your franchise development team verified independent contractor data. Every record is confirmed against state licensing boards. You know the business is real, the license is active, and the owner is reachable. Focus recruiting on the contractors most likely to convert.

Companies like Neighborly (One Hour, Mr. Rooter, Aire Serv), Authority Brands, Home Franchise Concepts need contractor data that goes beyond generic business listings. TradeBridge builds that data from state licensing boards and verifies every record before delivery.

Your data to keep

FAQ

You get the owner's name, a verified email address, a direct phone number, the company name and full address, their state license number with active status, and trade category. Service area and company size indicators are available as add-ons.
Verification runs in three stages: license status check against state boards, contact validation (email deliverability + phone carrier check), and company confirmation via business listing cross-reference. Only records that pass all three stages ship.
Twelve top-level trade categories and 26+ subtypes. HVAC covers residential, commercial, installation, maintenance, ductwork, refrigeration, and heat pump. Plumbing breaks into residential, commercial, drain cleaning, water heater, sewer, gas line, and backflow. Similar detail for electrical and roofing.
We build every list on demand. No stale database. License data is pulled from state boards at the time of your order, and contact details are verified before delivery. Most data is less than two weeks old at delivery.
All 50 states and DC. Some states publish licensing data online in real-time. Others update monthly or quarterly. We know the cadence for each state and time our pulls accordingly.
