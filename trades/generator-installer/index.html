About Generator Installer Contractor Data

Standby and portable generator installation, transfer switches, and service. TradeBridge sources generator installer contractor data from state licensing boards and verifies every record against 50+ sources. You get owner names, verified emails, direct phone numbers, active license status, and company details.

Generic B2B databases like ZoomInfo and Apollo group all contractors under broad categories like "construction" or "home improvement." They can't distinguish generator installer contractors from general contractors, handymen, or building material suppliers. TradeBridge segments by specific trade and subtype so your outreach reaches the right people.

What's included in every record

Standard fields: owner/operator name, email (deliverability-tested), direct phone, company name and address, state license number, active/inactive status, and trade classification. We can add additional data points for an additional fee.

How we verify generator installer data

We cross-check every contractor against their state licensing board for active status, validate email addresses through deliverability testing, verify phone numbers against carrier databases, and confirm company details against multiple public sources.
