About HVAC Maintenance Contractor Data

Preventive maintenance, tune-ups, and seasonal service contracts. TradeBridge sources hvac maintenance contractor data from state licensing boards and verifies every record against 50+ sources. You get owner names, verified emails, direct phone numbers, active license status, and company details.

Generic B2B databases like ZoomInfo and Apollo group all contractors under broad categories like "construction" or "home improvement." They can't distinguish hvac maintenance contractors from general contractors, handymen, or building material suppliers. TradeBridge segments by specific trade and subtype so your outreach reaches the right people.

What's included in every record

Each record includes the contractor's name, verified email, direct phone number, company name, address, license number, license status, and trade category. Custom fields available on request.

How we verify hvac maintenance data

Verification runs in three stages: license status check against state boards, contact validation (email deliverability + phone carrier check), and company confirmation via business listing cross-reference. Only records that pass all three stages ship.
