Roofing Data FAQ

You get the owner's name, a verified email address, a direct phone number, the company name and full address, their state license number with active status, and trade category. Service area and company size indicators are available as add-ons.
Verification runs in three stages: license status check against state boards, contact validation (email deliverability + phone carrier check), and company confirmation via business listing cross-reference. Only records that pass all three stages ship.
Each list is built to order using the most current state board records available. We don't recycle old data. Email and phone verification happens in the final 48 hours before delivery.
We cover every US state. Each state's contractor licensing board publishes records on its own schedule. We maintain pipelines for all 50 and adjust sourcing to match each board's refresh cycle.
You can filter by 12 trade categories and 26+ subspecialties. Want only residential HVAC contractors in Texas? Or commercial plumbers in the Northeast? We build that list. The segmentation comes from license classification data and trade focus analysis.
